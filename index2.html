Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian layered pasta dish with rich meat sauce and cheese. Perfect for family dinners and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook noodles according to package instructions; drain.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil, sauté garlic, cook beef until browned; drain fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer 5 minutes.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella in a baking dish; repeat.
  6. Top with Parmesan, cover with foil, bake 25 minutes.
  7. Remove foil, bake 10 more minutes until bubbly.
  8. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Pad Thai

Description

A flavorful stir-fried rice noodle dish from Thailand, combining sweet, sour, and savory elements with peanuts and lime.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz rice noodles
  • 2 tbsp tamarind paste
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 4 oz shrimp or chicken
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • ¼ cup chopped peanuts
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Soak noodles in warm water until pliable; drain.
  2. Mix tamarind, fish sauce, and sugar; set aside.
  3. Heat oil in wok, cook protein until done; push to side.
  4. Pour beaten eggs, scramble, then add noodles and sauce.
  5. Toss until noodles absorb sauce, add sprouts and onions.
  6. Serve with peanuts and lime wedges.

Pancakes

Description

Fluffy, golden-brown pancakes perfect for breakfast, topped with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Whisk in egg, milk, and butter until smooth.
  3. Heat griddle, pour ¼ cup batter per pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
  5. Serve warm with syrup.
